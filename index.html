Biography

Edison is a Ph.D. candidate at Northeastern University (Shenyang, China), started in 2011. He received his M.S. degree in computer science from Northeastern University in 2011. His research interests lie mostly in semi-structured data management and distributed database system.

He used to contribute to a subtitle group of US TV Shows named XTM. Now he is dedicating himself to his violin.
