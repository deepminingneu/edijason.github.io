Artificial Intelligence and Medical Data Analysis

1st semester, 2018-2019

Overview

The Artificial Intelligence and Medical Data Analysis briefly introduces artificial intelligence (AI) and its application over medical data analysis. This course mainly includes search problems, knowledge base, machine learning, etc.

We seek to provide students with new ideas about artificial intelligence, and the frontiers of IT industry. It's also guided by the latest academic research results, and let the participating students know about the frontier knowledge of artificial intelligence. Throughout the study and the task of this course, the students would master the basic ideas and technologies of solving various search problems, knowledge represenation and reasoning, planning, machine learning, etc.. This course, based on the background of biomedical engineering, aims to educate high-end talents for intelligent medical, precise medical, personal health-care with artificial intelligence, machine learning, data mining and analysis skills.
